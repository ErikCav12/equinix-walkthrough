Step 1
Sign in to partner portal

/login Sidebar: Dashboard, Invites, Templates
Step 2
Review dashboard overview
Scans stats cards: total assessments, average readiness score, how many need attention, completion rate. Stage distribution chart shows the portfolio breakdown.
Stats cards, stage distribution chart /dashboard
Step 3
Scan assessment pipeline

Toggle: Individuals / Clients view Needs attention filter, score sorting, "Mine" badges Client view: composite scores across all assessments per company /dashboard
Step 4
Open client detail
Clicks a row to open the detail drawer. In individual view: sees summary card with auto-generated insight, barrier heatmap, and 3-tab report (Diagnosis, Opportunities, Investment Case). In client view: sees composite scores across all assessments with an assessment selector to drill into any individual assessment.
Priority insight, barrier heatmap (security, integration, cost, expertise) 7-dimension radar chart, ranked opportunities, investment case Client view: assessment coverage bar + selector dropdown /dashboard (drawer)
Step 5
Generate live prototype
From the Opportunities tab, clicks "Prototype this" on a top-ranked opportunity. The platform generates a product spec from the assessment context, then builds a live interactive prototype. John reviews the spec and opens the prototype to show the client what an AI solution could look like.
Click "Prototype this" on opportunity card Generated product spec + live prototype URL /dashboard (prototype modal)

Take action

Copy link, archive, download PDF

Steps 1-5 are fully automated in the platform. Proposal generation is on the roadmap.

GSA to client

Assessment tracking link generated on /dashboard/invites. GSA copies and sends via email, Slack, or LinkedIn. Three link types: general, client-specific, person-specific.

Client to platform

5-step wizard captures structured responses. Data persisted to backend on first step (STARTED) and after AI generation (COMPLETED). No account required for client-specific links.

Platform to client

AI-generated readiness report with 7-dimension scoring, ranked opportunities, and investment case. Branded PDF download available. Client portal for returning users.

Platform to GSA

Scores, stages, use cases, budgets, and timelines surfaced in the pipeline table and detail drawer. Composite client view aggregates across multiple assessments. Live prototype generation from opportunities.

Next development priorities

Ordered by impact on the partner workflow.

Priority 1

Notes and activity log

Persist notes and flags to the backend. Archive action is already persisted. Notes enable team-wide visibility into client follow-up activity.

Priority 1

Email notifications

Notify GSAs when a client completes an assessment. Currently there is no notification; GSAs must check the dashboard manually.

Priority 1

Escalation workflow

"Flag for solution design" currently shows a toast but does not persist or notify the admin. Wire this to the notes system and surface flagged items in the admin view.

Priority 2

Industry benchmarks

Compare a client's scores against their industry average across all 7 dimensions. Gives the GSA an immediate conversation starter.

Priority 2

Aggregate analytics

Industry breakdown, use case distribution, and revenue pipeline funnel across the full portfolio. Currently the dashboard shows stage distribution only.

Priority 2

Proposal generation

Auto-generate a scoped proposal document from the assessment data, investment case, and prototype. Currently the GSA assembles this manually.
